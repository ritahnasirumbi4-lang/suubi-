DESTINATION

This park is famous for its diverse wildlife, including elephants, buffaloes, hippos, leopards, chimpanzees, and the unique tree-clambing lions found in the ishasha sector. visitors can enjoy game drives, boat cruises on the kazinga channel, bird watching, nature walks, and beautiful views of crater lakes and the rwenzori mountains.
